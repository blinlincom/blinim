# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Blinlin serves people who open the app many times a day to send private messages, manage group chats, handle friend requests, place audio/video calls, post moments, scan QR codes, and complete wallet or transfer flows. The main context is mobile-first, touch-first, quick-return communication, with web/iOS/desktop compatibility expected when the platform supports it.

## Product Purpose

The product is a commercial IM application built around real-time chat, group communication, calls, friend relationships, app-scoped backend switches, and account utilities. Success means a user can reach the current conversation, contact, group setting, call, wallet, or profile task quickly without visual noise or feature hunting.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, direct. The interface should feel like a serious communication tool rather than a social-media campaign page: quiet surfaces, clear hierarchy, familiar controls, strong interaction feedback, and a restrained but recognizable identity.

## Anti-references

Do not make it look like a marketing landing page, a decorative card gallery, a glassmorphism dashboard, a purple-gradient template, a fake luxury app, or an overstuffed admin panel. Avoid every-screen hero headers, nested cards, decorative blobs, over-rounded controls, and feature explanations inside the app UI.

## Design Principles

1. Put the conversation task first.
2. Group features by user intent, not by implementation module.
3. Keep personal chat and group chat structurally consistent.
4. Let backend switches remove or simplify UI instead of leaving disabled clutter.
5. Make every state legible: loading, empty, error, unread, muted, pinned, typing, read, calling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive controls. Keep touch targets at least 44dp, support reduced-motion-friendly transitions, avoid color-only status indicators, keep labels concise and specific, and preserve readable text at Android/iOS/Web/desktop font scaling.
